Same Day Dental Treatments

At our clinic, we offer same day dental treatments for patients who wish to get their dental problems fixed as swiftly as possible without going through long periods of treatment.

As soon as you come to us with your dental issues, we start your treatment process. Upon identifying the cause of your issue, our experienced dentists create a treatment plan. In the same day, we immediately begin with the treatment without losing any time.

Our state-of-the-art equipment and caring staff is at your disposal any time and are always at your service should you need them.

These are some of the treatments we offer in our “same day dental treatment” opportunities:

Same day dental implant treatment

Implant treatment involves replacing missing teeth with titanium implants. This implant serves as artificial foundation for dentures that are later added. Implants function and look like natural teeth.

Implant treatment might sometimes be a long process, as it requires delicate work. However, if the structure and condition of your teeth, gums and jawbone, implant treatment can be performed in just one day.

As soon as you arrive at our clinic, your treatment process begins. We make you go through certain check-ups, decide on a treatment plan, and then begin. Upon installing the implants in specified areas, we add the dentures or the crowns on the implant in the very same day.

Therefore, we avoid having you wait until receiving your dentures, in which time you’d be missing your teeth and thus probably be uncomfortable. Thanks to our same day dental implant treatment, you go through the bulk of your treatment in this one day and carry on your life with strong, stable and natural-looking new teeth.

Same day smile design

Smile design is trending cosmetic dentistry. For those who are constantly involved in social activities and interactions, especially due to their business, same day smile design allows you to obtain shining bright teeth with a rearranged, attractive smile.

In our smile design treatment, we arrange the teeth and gums in an aesthetically pleasing way that brings your face a confident and healthy smile.

Same day veneers

Veneers are the perfect cosmetic solution for discolored, stained or injured teeth. Thanks to our veneer treatment service, you can change the way your teeth looks in one day with a brief treatment process.

Veneers are made of different materials, such as porcelain or zirconia. According to your wishes, we immediately procure the necessary materials and perform a cosmetic procedure, at the end of which you acquire all shiny, white teeth.

Veneers are a great cosmetic solution for you if you aren’t happy with the way your teeth look. The procedure takes very little time and we guarantee your satisfaction.

Same day teeth whitening

Heavy smoking and tea-coffee consumption may cause stains on your teeth over time. To whiten your teeth and rid you of these stains, we offer you teeth whitening treatment.

Teeth whitening treatment involves clearing your teeth from any stains and discolorings by using a bleaching gel and laser, in what is called a Zoom technique. It is proven to be the most technologically advanced and effective teeth whitening procedure and it is performed in a very brief amount of time.

The whole process takes around an hour and it only requires you to avoid certain things like smoking or coffee for a few weeks after the treatment.

Frequently asked questions

Is sedation dentistry safe?

With proper patient selection and monitoring, yes. A medical assessment is required first.

Who needs sedation?

Patients with dental anxiety, gag reflex or long surgical procedures.

Can international patients request sedation in Istanbul?

Yes — tell us in advance so anaesthesia planning fits your travel dates.
